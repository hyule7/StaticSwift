Why StaticSwift exists.

Most UK small businesses are sold one of two things by web designers: a £30-a-month Wix subscription that locks them in forever, or a £3,000 bespoke build they can't afford. Neither serves a plumber in Letchworth or a dentist in Bromsgrove well.

StaticSwift exists because there's a middle ground: a custom-designed, fast-loading, mobile-first site that costs £149-£299 once, gets delivered in 24 hours, and is yours forever. No subscription. No platform lock-in. No technical debt.

Static sites have come a long way. They load instantly, they're cheap to host, they rank well on Google, and they don't require updates or patches. Pair that with a one-person studio willing to actually answer the phone, and you've got something most UK small businesses can't get anywhere else.
