What should a landscaper or gardener’s website include?

A landscaper or gardener’s website should show striking photos of finished gardens, patios and planting, list your services (design, paving, fencing, maintenance), and make it easy to request a quote. Landscaping is a considered, higher-value purchase, so a strong portfolio and clear, trustworthy contact win the bigger jobs.

Photos win landscaping jobs

Before-and-after garden transformations are compelling. A good gallery is the difference between a browse and a quote request.

Seasonal and maintenance work

Make clear whether you do one-off projects, regular maintenance, or both, so the right customers get in touch at the right time of year.
