What makes a good website for a painter and decorator?

A painter and decorator’s website is a portfolio first. Clean, bright photos of finished rooms and exteriors do most of the selling, backed by clear services (interior, exterior, wallpapering), real reviews, and an easy way to get a quote. Decorating is a taste and trust decision, so showing your work and being easy to deal with wins the job.

Lead with your best rooms

Homeowners choosing a decorator want to see finishes they would want in their own home. A tidy photo gallery is your strongest selling tool.

Make getting a quote easy

A simple call or message button, and the areas you cover, turns browsers into quote requests.
