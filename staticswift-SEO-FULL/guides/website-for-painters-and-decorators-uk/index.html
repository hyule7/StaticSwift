What should a painter and decorator’s website include?

Painting and decorating is a visual trade, so the website should lead with photos: clean, well-lit shots of finished rooms and exteriors, alongside reviews and the areas you cover. Decorators are chosen on taste and tidiness as much as price, so a site that shows your finish and makes it easy to enquire wins the jobs a Facebook page misses.
