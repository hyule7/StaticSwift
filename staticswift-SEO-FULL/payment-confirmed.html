Live in three minutes · The upload guide

Get the site live, on your own domain.

  1. 01
    Create a free Netlify account. Head to app.netlify.com and sign up — takes thirty seconds.
  2. 02
    Add new site → Deploy manually. From the dashboard top-right. Netlify gives you a drop zone.
  3. 03
    Drag your folder into the drop zone. The site is live the moment the upload finishes. You'll have a temporary “.netlify.app” URL right there.
  4. 04
    Connect your own domain. Site Settings → Domain Management → Add Custom Domain. Or the full upload guide walks you through it.
