Why scaffolders need this

The job goes to whoever the phone finds first.

Scaffolding is bought by two different customers: the homeowner who needs the chimney done and is mildly terrified of the whole subject, and the builder who needs a firm that turns up on programme with the right tickets. One website serves both if it is organised properly: TG20 compliance and CISRS cards stated for the trade, plain-English explanations and street photos for the householder, and a quote form that asks the four questions that actually price a job.
