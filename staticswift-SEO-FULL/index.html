The 24-hour clock is your preview. From the moment I receive your brief (after step 2), a working preview lands in your inbox within 24 hours — guaranteed. Going fully live (after your approval and revisions) typically takes another 24–48 hours. Total: preview in 24h, live within 72h.

What happens after launch

No surprise bills. Ever.

You own everything.

The code, the design, the domain. I email you a zip of the site on launch day. You can move it to any host, edit it yourself, or hire someone else to maintain it. No lock-in.

Hosting is optional.

£29/month if you want me to host it on a fast UK CDN with SSL and backups. Otherwise host it yourself on Netlify free, GitHub Pages, or anywhere — the files are yours.

Edits are easy.

Small text changes (prices, hours, services): I do them for free in the first 30 days. After that £30 for a batch, or learn it yourself — the code is hand-written, not a framework, so anyone can edit it.

Bigger updates later?

Add a booking system, gallery, blog, online store — same flat-fee pricing as a new site. No retainer, no monthly creep. Pay only when you need something built.
