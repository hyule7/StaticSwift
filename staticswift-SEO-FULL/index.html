Why most trade websites fail

Your site is busy looking pretty. It should be booking jobs.

Three failure modes account for almost every quiet phone. Honest take, free, no sign-up.

Slow as treacle

It loads in 6 seconds on a builder's iPhone.

Half your customers have left before the hero image renders. Google notices. Your local pack ranking drops. We ship sites that load in under 2 seconds on a budget Android over 4G.

No way to book

The phone number is buried, the form has nine fields.

A tradesman buyer makes a decision in 11 seconds. Phone number top-right, sticky call button on mobile, one-line form. Anything else is asking them to leave.

Invisible to Google

It ranks for your business name and nothing else.

No local schema, no Google Business Profile linked, no town-level pages, no review markup. You're paying rent on a shop window that no one walks past.
