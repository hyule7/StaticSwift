# BLITZ shift — all hands, one goal: a sale today

Harry hit "Start everyone working now". That is a direct order: every revenue
role works flat out, right now, toward getting one paying brief today. Drop
routine work. This is a sprint. Everything still goes to the approval queue so
Harry approves from his phone in one tap, but you fill that queue with the
highest-converting work you can produce, fast. Obey .claude/agents/_covenant.md.

Work these in parallel where you can, in this priority order (closest-to-money
first):

## 1. Reactivate warm leads (fastest path to a sale)
- **Lead Reactivation Specialist + Brief Chaser:** pull the CRM (get-clients).
  Anyone who started a brief and did not pay, used the website-check, or replied
  to outreach and went cold: draft a short, specific, warm message that asks for
  the brief now. Reference what they already told us. Queue as cs-reply or outreach.
- **Sales Closer:** any interested or high-intent reply gets a fast, human,
  specific reply that asks for the brief and offers the free 24-hour preview.

## 2. Preview-bait the hottest prospects ("I already built you this")
- **Preview Builder + Design Studio:** take the top scoring no-website prospects,
  build a real one-page preview from their public info in the Field Guide system,
  and draft the "I built you this" email with the live link. Highest-converting
  cold email in this niche. Always human-approved before send.

## 3. Outbound blitz (fill the top of the funnel)
- **Companies House Watcher + Website Checker + Contact Finder + Scorer + Writer
  + Sequencer:** run the outreach toolchain hard. Restock from Companies House,
  enrich, score, and draft 3-line personalised first emails opening on one true
  observation. Queue every one. Respect PECR and the suppression list. Aim to
  fill the queue with 30 to 50 ready-to-send, genuinely good emails.

## 4. Make the traffic we already have convert
- **Conversion Optimiser + Landing Page Tester:** check the funnel (analytics-self).
  If one specific change would lift form-submits today (a clearer CTA, a faster
  hero, a trust line), draft it for approval.
- **Paid Ads Manager + Ad Creative Designer:** if ads are running, brief one new
  creative aimed at the cheapest-cost-per-lead angle and queue it.

## 5. Trust and proof
- **Reputation Manager + Case Study Writer:** surface the strongest proof we have
  (Harrison Electrical, the guarantee) into anything going out today.

## Rules for the blitz
- Speed with standards: every message still passes the Covenant. No slop, no em
  dashes, every claim from facts.json. A fast bad email loses the sale and burns
  the address.
- Everything to the approval queue. Nothing sends itself. Tag each item so Harry
  can approve a whole batch in seconds.
- Log every meaningful action to agent-log so Harry watches the blitz live.
- Stop when the queue is full of high-quality, ready-to-approve work, or you
  approach the usage limit. Checkpoint and report what is queued.

## Done when
- The approval queue is stacked with reactivation messages, preview-bait,
  fresh outreach, and any conversion fix, all ready for one-tap approval.
- The live activity feed shows the whole revenue team worked.
