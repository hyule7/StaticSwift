About Tom

A Bristol electrician, twelve years on the tools.

I'm Tom Harrison. I've been wiring houses in Bristol for twelve years, ten of those on my own. Before that I served my apprenticeship with my uncle out in Yatton, who taught me that a tidy job done once is cheaper than a quick one done twice.

It's still me on the end of the phone. There's no call centre, no junior. When you book a callout I'm the one driving over. I started out doing emergency work because nobody else seemed to answer on Sunday evenings, and I've kept doing it for the same reason.

The promise is simple: I'll turn up when I said I would. I'll fix it properly. I'll charge what I quoted. And if anything's not right inside the year, I'll come back and put it right at no cost.
