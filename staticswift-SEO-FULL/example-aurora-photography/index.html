About

Hi, I'm Sarah.

I've photographed weddings since 2017. Before that, eight years in editorial portraiture for British and Scottish magazines. I work alone for elopements and small ceremonies, and bring a second photographer when the day deserves two pairs of eyes.

What I won't do: awkward group poses, fake confetti shots, golden-hour clichés, Instagram-preset edits. What I will do: spend the whole day with you, take a thousand frames you'll never see, and deliver the few hundred that will outlast every other thing about the day.

Questions you might have

Real answers.

How far in advance do I need to book?

+

Most weddings book 6–12 months out. I keep 2–3 spots open each year for shorter timelines and elopements.

What's the booking process?

+

Enquiry form → 30-minute call (free, no pressure) → I send a contract and 25% retainer invoice → date locked in. Final payment a month before the day.

Do you travel outside Edinburgh?

+

Yes — within 60 miles of Edinburgh is included. Further afield (Highlands, North-East England, Northern Ireland) is a flat £200 travel fee. International weddings are quoted individually.

Do you do engagement shoots?

+

Yes — £350 for two hours including 60+ edited images. Included free if you book The Full Day or The Weekend.

What if it rains?

+

It usually does. Some of my best work has been in proper Scottish weather — moody skies, candlelight indoors, umbrellas. I bring weather covers for the gear and a plan B for every shoot list.

When do we get the photos?

+

Sneak peeks (20 images) within 7 days. Full edited gallery within 4 weeks. Printable files and your fine-art book at the 6-week mark.
