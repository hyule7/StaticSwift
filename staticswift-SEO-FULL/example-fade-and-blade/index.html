About

Twelve years.
One chair.
One craft.

Mark Hennessy opened Fade & Blade in 2013 after eight years cutting at a Soho institution. The brief was simple: a proper barbershop. Sharp cuts, honest prices, time for a proper chat. No "experience packages." No upsells. Just a chair, a mirror, and someone who knows what they're doing.

Today there are two chairs, two master barbers, and a small waiting bench. We cut around 2,400 heads a year. We don't take walk-ins after 5pm — but we'll always make time for someone who's run out of options before a wedding.
