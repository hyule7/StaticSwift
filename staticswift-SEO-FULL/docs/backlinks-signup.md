## 5. The scalable one (compounds with every sale)
- Put a small "Website by StaticSwift" credit link in the footer of every client
  site you build (with their permission). Each live client site becomes a
  relevant backlink. Over a year this is your strongest, most natural link
  source, and it is free.

## 6. Digital PR / content links (slower, high value)
- Write one genuinely useful guest piece for a UK trades blog or local business
  publication ("why tradespeople lose jobs to a bad website").
- Get listed in "best web designers in Manchester" roundup articles (email the
  authors, offer a free preview as the hook).
- Answer questions on Reddit (r/smallbusinessuk), trade Facebook groups, and
  Quora where a website genuinely helps - link only when it truly adds value.
