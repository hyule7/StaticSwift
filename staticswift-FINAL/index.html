FAQ

Questions answered.

Can I edit the site myself?

Your website is standard HTML and opens in any text editor — you are welcome to make changes yourself. Most clients prefer to just email us and we handle all changes free of charge, forever. Any questions email support@staticswift.co.uk — always free, always a real person.

What if I do not like the design?

Every site comes with one free revision included. If you are not happy after that, you get a full refund with no questions asked. We are confident in our work — we just want you to be happy. Any questions email support@staticswift.co.uk — always free, always a real person.

Will my site rank on Google?

Every site is built with SEO best practices baked in — correct meta tags, schema markup, fast load times, and clean semantic HTML. These are all strong foundations for ranking. SEO results depend on your niche and location, but your site will be technically sound from day one. Any questions email support@staticswift.co.uk — always free, always a real person.

What hosting platforms can I use?

Your site works on any hosting platform — Netlify (free, we recommend this), GitHub Pages, Cloudflare Pages, cPanel, SiteGround, or any standard web host. We include a step-by-step upload guide with every delivery. Any questions email support@staticswift.co.uk — always free, always a real person.

Is it truly one payment?

Yes, completely. You pay once and the site is yours forever. No platform fees, no renewal costs, no surprises. The only ongoing cost is your domain name — typically £10 to £15 per year for a .co.uk. Any questions email support@staticswift.co.uk — always free, always a real person.

How is this different from Wix?

With Wix you never own anything — stop paying and your site disappears. StaticSwift gives you the actual HTML file, so you own your website outright and can host it anywhere for free. Over three years you would spend over £500 on Wix for a site you never own. StaticSwift is £149, once, yours forever. Any questions email support@staticswift.co.uk — always free, always a real person.

What if I need changes later?

Email support@staticswift.co.uk and we will sort it for you, free of charge, forever. We do not charge for updates or maintenance. Existing clients always get priority. Any questions email support@staticswift.co.uk — always free, always a real person.

Do I need technical knowledge?

None whatsoever. Fill in our 2-minute form, we build it, you approve it, we send the files. We also include a simple upload guide and will help you get it live if needed. Any questions email support@staticswift.co.uk — always free, always a real person.
