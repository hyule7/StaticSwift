Who We Are

FIVE YEARS IN
THE NORTHERN
QUARTER

Fade and Blade opened in 2019 in the heart of Manchester's Northern Quarter. We built this place on a simple premise — do good work, be straight with people, and make sure every client walks out looking better than when they came in.

No appointments needed. No fifteen-minute waits while someone finishes their coffee. You walk in, we get you sorted, you leave looking sharp. That is the whole business model and it works.

  • Walk-ins always welcome — no booking required
  • Open seven days a week including bank holidays
  • Five years and 500+ satisfied clients in Manchester
  • Qualified and experienced barbers — no trainees
